Next, log in to CALC

To log in, click the login button at the bottom of the CALC homepage or visit https://calc.gsa.gov/auth/login. You'll be taken to Cloud.gov and asked to enter your GSA login credentials

Once logged in, click "Add price data"

After you log in, you should see an option to "Add price data" in the navigation. Click it to begin.

Then, upload your price list

You will be asked for very basic details around the contract (e.g. contract number, schedule name, business size, etc..) When you have filled that out, simply upload your contract to CALC.

You're done! It's that easy.

You should receive an e-mail when your price list has been approved. If you have any questions or suggestions, please reach out to us at calc@gsa.gov.
